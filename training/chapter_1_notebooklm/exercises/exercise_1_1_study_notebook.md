# Exercise 1.1: Study Materials Notebook

## Objective
Create a NotebookLM notebook for the XYZ-2026-001 study and use it to extract key information from the protocol and SAP.

## Time: 15 minutes

## Prerequisites
- Google account (logged in)
- Chrome browser
- PDF files:
  - `study_data/protocol_excerpt_dm.pdf`
  - `study_data/sap_excerpt_dm.pdf`

---

## Steps

### Step 1: Create the Notebook

1. Go to https://notebooklm.google.com
2. Click **"New Notebook"**
3. Name it: **"XYZ-2026-001 DM Programming"**

### Step 2: Upload Study Documents

1. Click **"Add Source"** (or the + icon)
2. Select **"Upload"** and choose `protocol_excerpt_dm.pdf`
3. Wait for processing (30-60 seconds) -- you'll see a spinner
4. Once processed, click **"Add Source"** again
5. Upload `sap_excerpt_dm.pdf`
6. You should now see 2 sources listed in the left panel

### Step 3: Explore the Source Guides

1. Click on the **protocol** source in the left panel
2. Review the auto-generated **Source Guide**:
   - Read the **Summary** section
   - Look at the **Suggested Questions**
   - Browse the **Table of Contents** outline
3. Repeat for the **SAP** source

### Step 4: Ask Questions

In the chat interface, ask the following questions one at a time. After each answer, note the citation numbers and verify at least one citation by clicking on it.

3. Verify the note appears in the **Notes** panel
